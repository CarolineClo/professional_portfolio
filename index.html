About me...

Hello, I'm Caroline and I'm studying multimedia design at KEA in Copenhagen. I’m currently in my third semester and focusing in frontend design, expanding my skills in HTML, CSS, and Javascript. I’ve also been gaining knowledge of the Adobe suite including illustrator and Photoshop and creating wireframes in figma and XD. I’m naturally curious and creative and am passionate about finding useful and good looking solutions. I appreciate the importance of a positive attitude and the power of a great team. Conclusion...?

I found these assignments to be enjoyable as I learned some new techniques and gained new competencies. I have learned how to create a colour pallet which is a skill I’m constantly practicing, with the help of excellent resources such as huemint.com, and I have learned how to take in colour pallets from websites I find. I utilised the major third type scale for font hierarchy in my assignments, and now enjoy discovering new fonts and scales to try together. I have also learned the importance of using sans serif fonts for body text. I very much enjoyed my introduction to animation and I’m excited to continue discovering more possibilities, I have taken some time to understand the scrolling animation and although I’m not satisfied with my final result in this assignment, I’m very happy to carry on with these experiments and look forward to creating more beautiful solutions.
